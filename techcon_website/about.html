History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software developers and has since grown into one of the largest technology conferences globally. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees and hundreds of talks on cutting-edge innovations.

Mission

Our mission is to connect technologists from around the world, foster innovation, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. TechCon champions diversity, open knowledge sharing, and ethical technological progress.
